My name is Aleia Walker.

I write copy to help businesses reach their audiences.

And I write code to make them look gorgeous.

You’re on my site which means that I think you’re awesome. You’re reading (attempting to read) my bio which means you are currently my favorite person. Now I will get to the point.


I was born in South Florida but grew up outside of Atlanta.

I have a deep connection to my Jamaican heritage.

I returned to South Florida to get a B.A. and a M.A. from the University of Miami.

I spent six magical months in Washington, D.C. at a think tank writing and editing stories on the Caribbean.


After graduating, I began my career as a freelance content creator helping businesses reach their audiences.

This year I added web design + development to my skillset.


So now I call myself a woman in tech.

And this woman is seeking the opportunity to join an amazing team and do amazing, purposeful work.


If you still want to know more about me take a look at some of my work or my resume, my LinkedIn, or tweet me.
